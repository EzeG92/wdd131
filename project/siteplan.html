Overview

Purpose

"Make it easier for parents and caregivers to monitor the development and care of their children in detail and organized from birth to the first years of age, offering intuitive tools to record relevant medical information, manage appointments with the pediatrician, receive personalized recommendations, and consult clearly the vaccination schedule, all in a safe environment and accessible from any device."
